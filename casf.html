Who We Are

We continue to evolve this website and ask others to do the same in order to connect the UD Soccer community, to promote the program and strengthen the bond we share.

Community

The University of Dallas is liberal arts school based on the ancient Greek and Roman cultures. Each student spends a significant amount of time studying these ancient cultures in the "Core." This allows students of every major to share common classes and unite the student body as a whole. This creates strong bonds among students in the classroom. Extending beyond the classroom is the thletic program. Driven mainly by the passion of the players and coaches UD has helped us form bonds that will last a lifetime.
